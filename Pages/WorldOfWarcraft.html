World of Warcaft

In 2011 I set my first step into add-on creation for World of Warcraft. It started out as an ugly duckling, but I was determined to stick to it, and as it improved in quality, so did my skills. In the years since, I've created more projects, experimented with new things, and learned a lot. But most importantly I found a passion. By sharing my add-ons with others, I learned the importance of feedback. With every add-on you create something, get feedback, and learn to use it to improve both the product and your skills. When creating an add-on I take it upon myself to make it fit in with the default UI as much as possible. Both as an extra challenge, and to not take away from the player's immersion by them looking out of place.
